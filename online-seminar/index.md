The seminar takes place online twice a year: 
1) **the second Monday of November** at 15:00-17:00 (CEST), 9:00-11:00 (EDT), 19:30-21:30 (IST), 1:00-3:00 (+1 day AEST)
2) **the last Friday of April** at 9:00-11:00 (CEST), 3:00-5:00 (EDT), 12:30-14:30 (IST), 17:00-19:00 (AEST)
Each session is constituted of two talks of 45 miniutes.
